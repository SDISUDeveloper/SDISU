Who We Are

We are Riptides. United by our love of the beautiful game, we are the architects of unity, the stewards of positive change, and the creators of connection. Together, we celebrate the diversity of San Diego and work to elevate each community and one another.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple but powerful:

  • Community Unity and Diversity: Bringing people of all backgrounds together through soccer.
  • Local Heritage and Identity: Infusing our city’s unique culture into everything we do.
  • Community Impact and Engagement: Driving positive change through service and collaboration.
  • Empowerment through Shared Knowledge: Deepening our understanding and joy of the game.
  • Passion in Action: Filling every stadium with energy and love for our team and city.

How To Get Involved

Connect: Follow us on Instagram @Riptides and join the Union Discord to stay up to date on events and news.
Engage: Participate in charity drives, social events, and volunteer opportunities.
Represent: Wear your Riptides colors proudly and bring your enthusiasm to every match and event. Let’s make waves together!

Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and empowering environment for everyone. If you ever feel your experience doesn’t reflect these values, we want to hear from you. Please reach out and let us know how we can do better.
